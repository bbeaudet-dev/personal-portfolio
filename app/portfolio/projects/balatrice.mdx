_A Balatro-inspired dice-rolling game with satisfying gameplay and a variety of items and upgrades to collect_

## Overview

Balatrice combines elements of traditional card and dice games with modern roguelike progression, where players build powerful combinations and face increasingly difficult challenges. Synergy and emergent complexity arise from the vast collection of items, consumables, power-ups, and other scaling elements.
