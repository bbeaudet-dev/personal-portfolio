## Overview

Started **February 21, 2019**, this was a hands-on embedded project: wire up the hardware, drive a small screen, read joystick input, and implement Snake end-to-end. We also hooked up **speakers** and wrote **simple tunes inspired by Mario** that played while you played the game.

## What we built

- Breadboard circuit with microcontroller, display, and input hardware
- Joystick-based movement and on-screen Snake gameplay
- Audio output with programmed music for an arcade feel
- Iteration and debugging entirely on the bench (no PC game engine—just hardware and firmware)
